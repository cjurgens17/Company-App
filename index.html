Have a look at our most iconic pools!

Crystal Lagoon

The Crystal Lagoon is a breathtaking oasis that evokes a sense of tranquility and serenity with its clear turquoise water and sparkling white sand beach. The pool's infinity edges seemingly blend into the surrounding landscape, creating an illusion of a boundless water body.

Enchanted Cove

The Enchanted Cove is a pool that evokes a magical and mystical feel, and the pool lives up to its name with its beautiful rock formations and natural waterfalls that create an ambiance of serenity and calm.

Sunset Oasis

The Sunset Oasis features a spectacular panoramic view of the setting sun. The pool's warm and inviting waters create a peaceful haven, while the surrounding palm trees and tropical foliage offer a perfect retreat for anyone looking to escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

Imperial Baths

The Imperial Baths is a grandiose Roman-style pool adorned with ornate statues and intricate mosaics. The pool's classical architecture and opulent design harken back to the days of ancient Rome.
